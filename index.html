Jarvis is a National Level Technical Symposium presented by Department of Mechatronics of Chennai Institute of Technology. JARVIS aims at bringing the technologies from science fiction right into the real world. It is an amazing platform for the students from all over the country to explore engineering through competitions, workshops and much more...
